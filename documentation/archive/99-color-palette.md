# Fonts

1. Inter
The default choice for modern SaaS, dashboards, and product UIs. Extremely readable at small sizes, looks premium without feeling trendy, and works beautifully in dense interfaces. Widely considered the “modern Helvetica for apps.” Available on Google Fonts and optimized for screens.

2. Geist Sans
Very popular in the React / Next.js ecosystem (created by Vercel). Feels like a cleaner, slightly more refined Inter with a modern SaaS aesthetic. Excellent for startup/product UIs and increasingly common in 2025–2026. Easy to use and free.

3. Manrope
A more elegant and slightly more branded-looking alternative to Inter. Popular for tech products because it feels clean, modern, and friendly while still professional. Great if you want something a bit more distinctive without hurting readability. Available via Google Fonts.

4. Plus Jakarta Sans
Trending heavily in UI/product design. Softer and friendlier than Inter, excellent for polished consumer-facing apps. Feels modern and premium without being overly geometric. Available on Google Fonts.

5. DM Sans
A safe modern UI font with excellent readability and a slightly warmer feel than Inter. Often used in fintech, startups, and marketing-heavy SaaS products where a clean but approachable vibe matters. Google Fonts-ready.
